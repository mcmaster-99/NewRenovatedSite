    Explain how to add third party device/findable using mobile app

    Inlo uses the technology in your smart phone to detect third party devices. You must use your Inlo Mobile App to add a device. You will hold your smart phone close to the device you would like to add, and follow the instructions.

    (1) Open Mobile App.
    (2) Click "Add Third Party Device"
    (3) Follow instructions of phone
